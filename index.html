6 Key Facts About CA Wildfires

1. Fire Season Lengthening

California's fire season has extended by 75 days compared to historical averages. (Source: NBC NEWS)

2. Urban Interface Risk

Over 2 million homes now sit in high-risk wildfire zones. (Source: Cal Matters)

3. Climate Change

Average temperatures have increased 3°F since 1900, drying vegetation, combined with the Santa Ana winds carying embers spreading the fires more quickly. (Source: Scripps)

4. Acres Burned

Over 50,000 acres burned in 2025 so far. (Source: Cal Matters)

5. Economic Impact

The economic impact of the 2025 Los Angeles Wildfires range from $76 billion to $131 billion. (Source: Anderson)

6. How You Can help

Visit California Volunteers for the many ways you can help those impacted by wildfires. (California Volunteers)
